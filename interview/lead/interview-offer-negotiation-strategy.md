# Interview Offer Negotiation Strategy for QA Leads

## What

Practical guide for discussing offer terms and role scope after technical rounds.

## Why

Strong candidates lose value when negotiation is unstructured.
A prepared strategy improves compensation and role-fit outcomes.

## How

### Preparation

- define minimum acceptable package and target range;
- capture measurable impact examples (quality metrics, delivery wins);
- list non-salary priorities: role scope, team size, tech stack, growth path.

### Negotiation flow

1. confirm role expectations and success criteria;
2. align compensation with responsibility level;
3. discuss bonus/equity/review cycle and probation terms;
4. ask for final offer in writing with timeline.

### Risk checks

- unclear ownership boundaries;
- mismatch between title and scope;
- unrealistic delivery expectations without staffing support.

## Q&A

**Q1: When should compensation discussion happen?**  
After value alignment is clear and before final acceptance.

**Q2: What should be negotiated besides base salary?**  
Bonus model, review cycle, signing terms, flexibility, and growth path.

**Q3: Best way to justify requested level?**  
Use concrete outcomes and domain impact rather than generic market statements.
